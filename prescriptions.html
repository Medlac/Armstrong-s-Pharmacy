Prescription Dispensing

Why Let Us Dispense Your Prescription?

Here at Armstrong's Pharmacy, we undestand what means most to patients and their prescriptions.
This is why we aim to dispense prescriptions in a timely manner, while ensuring every prescription is correct and safe.
We accommodate for patient's preferences on the brands of their medication while doing everything in our power to get you the medication you need.

How to Register for our Prescription Service

For your prescriptions to be electronically sent to us, submit your details to us via the 'Nomination Form' on this page. Alternatively you can ring us, or nominate us using the NHS app.

Prescription Ordering

To arrange your re-order, contact us via telephone, instore or by using the NHS app. Using the NHS app is another way to communicate with Armstrong's Pharmacy and organise your medication better.

The NHS App

Managing your health, prescriptions, appointments and chosen pharmacy can all be done within the NHS app. We strongly recommend using it if you are able to do so.
