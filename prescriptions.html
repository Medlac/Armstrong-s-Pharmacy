Prescriptions

Here at Armstrong's Pharmacy, dispensing prescriptions is at the centre of our pharmacy business. We pride ourselves on providing a quick, efficient and convenient prescription service. We also to endeavour to do eveything in our power to get you the medication you are prescribed or to fulfil any preference you may have regarding your medicines.

How to Register to our Prescription Service

To arrange for your prescriptions to be electronically sent to us, submit your details to us via the 'Nomination Form'. Alternatively you can ring us, or nominate us using the NHS app.
